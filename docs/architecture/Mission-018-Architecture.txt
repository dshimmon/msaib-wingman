============================================================
RESPONSIBILITY BOUNDARY
=======================

OPENAI

* Interprets natural-language questions
* Produces structured retrieval instructions
* Creates numerical embeddings
* Reasons over organized context
* Produces the final answer

WINGMAN OS

* Creates structured knowledge
* Preserves metadata and sources
* Stores concepts and records
* Maintains persistent concept memory
* Creates and stores knowledge embeddings
* Executes retrieval plans deterministically
* Performs exact record filtering
* Compares semantic similarity
* Rejects weak semantic matches
* Ranks evidence
* Builds context for OpenAI

============================================================
MISSION 018 OUTCOME
===================

Before Mission 018:

Wingman primarily found knowledge through exact wording,
structured records, and concept memory.

After Mission 018:

Wingman can also find relevant knowledge by meaning when
the user’s wording differs from the source.
